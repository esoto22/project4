Hispanics



Hispanics are stereotyped to be all mexican when there is more than just one country. We are not all the same. We are nicaraguan, peruvian, spaniard, brazilian, honduran, and so many other things don't stereotype us all to be Mexican. We don't all play soccer either or are all gardeners or maids. Our culture is beautiful so stop making fun of it and trying to replicate it in a disgusting way. Stop.




The world would be so much better if society stops putting people into categories and containers full of stereotypes. Break prejudice and be the change you want to see in the world.
