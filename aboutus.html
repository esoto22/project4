About us

We are an inclusive, non-profit organization that wants to get rid of stereotypes for the greater good. Stereotypes break people down into categories of certain subjects that aren't even true. They cost people their jobs at certain times, their status in society, their mental health, and sometimes even their lives. We are here to inform people of what they are doing when they stereotype someone or put them into societies categories. Help us break prejudice.
